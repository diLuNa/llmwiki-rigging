## Research Themes
Hyperelastic material simulation, constitutive models for soft bodies, production FEM solvers, subspace simulation. Strong focus on mathematical foundations with direct production application.

## Notes
Developed stable Neo-Hookean formulation while at Pixar; co-authored the Dynamic Deformables course with David Eberle. Now at Yale. The course notes + open-source code are the best practical reference for production FEM simulation.
