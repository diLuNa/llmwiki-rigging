## Research Themes
Production-scale physics-based facial animation. Developed ILM's anatomy-based face simulation pipeline from MRI scan to production blendshape rig. All papers are joint with Ron Fedkiw (Stanford advisor). Deployed the system on *Kong: Skull Island* (2017).
