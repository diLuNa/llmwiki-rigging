## Research Themes
Numerical methods for deformable solids and fluids, FEM simulation, biomechanical simulation, MPM (Material Point Method). Developed the quasistatic FEM flesh engine (Teran 2005) that underlies ILM's face pipeline. Later known for MPM snow simulation (*Frozen*).
