## Research Themes
Neural parametric head models and 4D facial reconstruction. Core contributor to the NPHM → NPGA → Face Anything lineage at TUM/Nießner lab. Work spans implicit neural representations of head geometry, Gaussian-based animatable avatars, and feed-forward 4D reconstruction with canonical correspondence prediction.

## Notes
Co-first author on NPHM (CVPR 2023) and first author on NPGA (SIGGRAPH Asia 2024). Also co-created the NeRSemble multi-view dataset used across the lab's benchmarks.
