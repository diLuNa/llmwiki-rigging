## Research Themes
Geometric processing for production character pipelines. Discrete differential geometry on polygonal meshes. Elasticity-based deformation tools (Kelvinlets, Somigliana coordinates, cage deformation). Character rigging systems (curvenet rigging, sculpt processing, garment tools). Bridges rigorous mathematical foundations with practical artist workflows.

## Notes
Most prolific author in this wiki. Long-term Pixar collaborator/employee; also collaborated with Mathieu Desbrun (Caltech/Inria/Ecole Polytechnique) and Doug L. James (Stanford). The Kelvinlet and curvenet lineages are the two major research threads in this collection.
