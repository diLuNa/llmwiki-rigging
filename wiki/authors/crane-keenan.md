## Research Themes
Discrete differential geometry, geometry processing, mathematical physics applied to graphics. Recurring tools: Gauss map, Morse theory, exterior calculus, heat methods, conformal geometry. Known for the **heat method for geodesics**, **conformal parameterization**, and a widely-used CMU graduate course on discrete differential geometry.
